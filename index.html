We require each guest have - and be prepared to show - a valid unexpired government-issued identification upon booking, as they will be given to Punta Cana Village security to allow access to the complex.

Terrazas del Mar is a secure, relaxing, building complex located right in the heart of Punta Cana village. Security is ensured by tracking the people who are allowed into the premises. Upon Airbnb booking, I provide Punca Cana Village the names of the guests who will be staying on my property and thus allow my guests access to the building complex.

Car entrance is through Amapola street (please look at diagram below):

Our condo has 1 private designated parking available (#7403) for our Airbnb guests, as well as access to guest parking (which is shared across the entire complex).

Please ask for more information prior to your arrival. Check this diagram for info:

Check-in is between 2pm and 4pm unless otherwise agreed to via AirBnB messaging. We fully clean and turn-over the property daily. That requires time to ensure it is done to your satisfaction.

You will be issued a code for the electronic lock box at the condo entrance which contains the apartment key. This code is valid during the agreed-upon check-in and check-out times (normally 3pm - 10am).

When your stay is completed and you are ready to check-out please do the following:

  • Checkout is at 12:00pm.
  • Leave used towels on the floor.
  • Take your trash out before you leave including bottles and food waste.
  • Double-check for personal belongings.
  • Turn off AC/lights.
  • Close and lock all windows and doors.
  • Leave apartment key inside lock box after locking main door on your way out.
  • Notify Sarah (owner) of departure.
  • As previously agreed upon booking, no smoking permitted - even on the balcony. Extra cleaning fees to remove odor and stains may be applied.
  • No drugs allowed in or around the property.
  • As previously agreed upon booking, pets are not allowed inside the condo.

PLEASE NOTE: Children friendly does NOT mean the area is CHILD-PROOF. It is the responsibility of the guardian/parent to monitor their children's actions AT ALL TIMES. The apartment unit is located on the 4th floor with a balcony. The spiral staircase inside the apartment can be dangerous for unsupervised toddlers. Keep a close eye on your kids while being on the property.

Please be energy conscious as it helps keep the cost of this property down. Turn off unused lights. AC is to be used only when someone is resting. When you are not in the property, everything must be turned off.

As previously agreed upon booking, 7 towels will be provided for guests: 5 for bathroom use ONLY (white with GM initials), and 2 pool use ONLY (orange with GM initials). Please do not take towels to the beach.

As previously agreed upon booking, please acknowledge that from occasionally, we may leave consumables such as condiments or snacks left behind by previous guests if they are not empty. We try our very best to ensure that it is not expired or stale. HOWEVER, ultimately, it is up to the guests' discretion and inspection to consume these items if they wish. The host does not take any responsibility for the consumables.

Cooking utensils are there for guest usage.
